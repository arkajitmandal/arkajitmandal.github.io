Extended Hückel App

In my  leisure time, I like developing apps for understanding chemical concepts. Recently, I have been working to make a web-based cheap electronic structure app. I used the Semi-empirical Extended Huckel approach to compute the molecular electronic structure.  

Calistry.org

Made this website back in 2013, during the early years of my B.Sc. Has about a hundred calculators related to different topics in a typical undergrad/high-school chemistry.  Gets ~1 million visitors each year.  To my surprise these tools were cited in several research papers and in a book about Fish! ¯\_(ツ)_/¯
