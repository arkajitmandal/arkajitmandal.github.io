An On-the-Fly Quantum Dynamics Approach

We developed an on-the-fly quantum dynamics approach, the Quasi-Diabatic (QD) Propagation Scheme, to resolve a long-standing incompatibility challenge, that is combining any diabatic quantum dynamics approach with the outputs of an electronic structure method provided in the adiabatic representation.  The QD approach has enabled us to simulate and gain mechanistic insights into complex reactions like the proton coupled electron transfer reaction. Our method extends the applicability of both the electronic structure methods as well as the existing diabatic quantum dynamics approaches.



Molecular QED and Polariton Chemistry

In polaritonic  chemistry the quantum nature of photons is harnessed to enable new chemical reactivities. We developed new theoretical tools, theoretical frameworks and analytical theories to investigate new chemical reactivities enabled by coupling quantized radiation to molecular systems. With these tools we provide time-dependent mechanistic insights and discover new ways of enabling & controlling chemical reactivities.
