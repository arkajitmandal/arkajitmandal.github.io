Extended Hückel App

Headed the development of this application with an undergrad at UofR.  This is a web-based cheap electronic structure app aimed at undergrads studying chemistry. I used the Semi-empirical Extended Huckel approach to compute the molecular electronic structure.  

Calistry.org

Headed the development of this website which was made with my friends during our undergrad. Has about a hundred calculators on different topics in a typical undergrad/high-school chemistry.  Gets ~1 million visitors each year.  To my surprise these tools were cited in several research papers and in a book about Fish! ¯\_(ツ)_/¯ 

An Intro Comp. Chem. Course  

Made an introductory course on Computational Chemistry for high school students.  Has been teaching every summer at the University of Rochester (Upward Bound summer program) since 2017. The high school kids were able to perform molecular dynamics simulation of water molecules passing through a Carbon Nanotubes.
